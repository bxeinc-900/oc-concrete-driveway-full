## Why Concrete Over Asphalt?
- **Longevity**: Cement is a sturdier material that degrades slower than tar-based asphalt.
- **Maintenance**: Concrete requires significantly less upkeep and fewer repairs.
- **Value**: While asphalt may be 30% cheaper initially, concrete's lifespan makes it more cost-effective.
